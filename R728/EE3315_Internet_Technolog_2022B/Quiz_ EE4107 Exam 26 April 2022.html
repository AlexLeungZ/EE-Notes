    Answer all the following four parts.  

    1. What is tokenization? Discuss how it can enhance the security of online transactions.        (4 marks)
    2. Bank of China (HK) has launched a mobile app using "tokens" to authenticate transactions more conveniently. Explain in what ways tokenization help to increase security during authenticating transactions. (3 marks)
      • You may refer to the following link for more operation information on the mobile app. https://www.bochk.com/dam/more/bochk_mtoken_faq_en.pdf (Links to an external site.)
    3. Five potential vulnerabilities are affecting mobile payment systems, which are (I) man-in-the-middle attacks, (II) replay attacks, (III) repudiation, (IV) impersonation, and (V) unauthorized access. Their meanings are briefly described below. Suggest two potential vulnerabilities that tokenization may increase the security of mobile payment. (4 marks)
      1. A man in the middle attack: a general term for when a perpetrator positions himself in a conversation between a user and an application by either eavesdropping or impersonating one of the parties. As a result, it appears as if a normal exchange of information is underway.
      2. A replay attack is a network attack linked to an external site such that valid data transmission is maliciously or fraudulently repeated or delayed.
      3. A repudiation attack happens when an application or system does not adopt controls to track and log users' actions properly, thus permitting malicious manipulation or forging the identification of new actions
      4. An impersonation attack uses social engineering and personalization to trick someone into unwittingly transferring money to a fraudulent account or sharing sensitive data with cybercriminals.
      5. Unauthorized access means a person gains logical or physical access without permission to a network, system, application, data, or another resource.
    4. Asymmetric cryptography is another way to enhance security during an online transaction. What are the key differentiating features between asymmetric cryptography and tokenization in improving security in an online transaction? (4 marks)
